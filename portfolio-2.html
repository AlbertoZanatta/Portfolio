Viking Quest is a 2D action game in which players guide an unnamed Viking on his quest to collect the powerful magic crystals scattered around his world. Each level is filled with enemies, traps, environmental hazards, doors to unlock. Once players collect a crystal, the layout of the level suddenly changes as they need to backtrack to the starting location.

My contributions

  • Implemented the Viking's in game controls, animations and combat abilities - players can parry incoming attacks with a wooden shield and attack enemies using their axe or by throwing deadly knives against them
  • Implemented a basic inventory system for storing and using potions, keys and knives
  • Developed multiple enemy types each with their own attack patterns and FSM-based AI
  • Designed the first level and the ice section of the second one
  • Implemented most of the traps and environmental hazards
  • Implemented the game's checkpoint system
