Stranded is a top down, twin-stick shooter in which players control an astronaut stranded on a hostile planet. They need to collect multiple resources, build defenses and equip powerful weapons to fend off waves of incoming hostile alien creatures and protect the nearby spaceship.

My contributions

  • Designed the tutorial and translated tutorial texts into English;
  • Implemented the astronaut's in game controls, movements and combat abilities;
  • Designed and implemented the weapon system and the three different weapon types currently available (laser rifle, missile launcher and combat rifle);
  • Designed and implemented the building system and defensive tools currently available (turrets, laser walls and proximity mines);
  • Implemented the turrets' AI using Beahvior Trees;
  • Implemented the three-fold energy system.
